BATMAN

Batman, created by artist Bob Kane and writer Bill Finger, is a fictional superhero appearin g in DC Comics. Debuting in 1939, Batman is the alter ego of Bruce Wayne, a billionaire playboy and philanthropist who witnessed his parents' murder, inspiring him to fight crime in Gotham City. Trained to physical and intellectual perfection, Batman relies on his detective skills, gadgets, and martial arts prowess. With no superpowers, he embodies the human potential to overcome adversity in the relentless pursuit of justice.
